Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Who We Are

PilotForge LLC ("PilotForge," "we," "us") operates the PilotForge schedule management service. We are an independent company not affiliated with any airline, union, or scheduling provider.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PilotForge, we may collect:

  • Phone number: Your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify your account
  • Schedule data: Flight schedules, trip information, and related documents you upload
  • Messages: Queries and conversations with the PilotForge assistant
  • Profile information: Base, fleet, seat, and years of service (provided by you)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide schedule analysis and compliance checking
  • Calculate pay estimates and optimization suggestions
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our service

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Isolation

Each pilot's data is stored in an isolated environment. Your schedule data, conversations, and profile information are not visible to or shared with other users.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. We use Supabase for data storage with row-level security policies ensuring per-user data isolation.

6. Third-Party Services

PilotForge uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • WhatsApp Business API (Meta): Message delivery
  • Supabase: Secure data storage
  • Anthropic (Claude): AI-powered schedule analysis

These services process data only as needed to provide the PilotForge service and are bound by their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw from the service at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via WhatsApp.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@pilotforge.app.
