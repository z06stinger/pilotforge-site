"What's my FDP limit for a 3-leg day reporting at 0600?"
Per FAR §117.13 Table B, acclimated, 0500-0559 report: 3 segments = 12.0h FDP limit. Extension available per §117.19 up to 2 additional hours with PIC/SIC agreement.
"Can I fit a 6-day trip starting May 20 into my bucket?"
Day-by-day analysis: your bucket peaks at 98.0h on May 22. The trip fits — 2h margin. Old A157 hours drop off May 2-7, creating room as the new trip's block accumulates.
"What are my GS options on X-days May 20-27?"
75.3h bucket room on May 20 → fits any trip. Best play: 6-day reporting May 20 at ~$9,400 GS pay on top of guarantee. Or stack two 3-days for flexibility.
"I was rerouted yesterday. Today they're trying to reroute me again instead of returning me. Legal?"
Per PWA Section 23 L., after a reroute the Company must return you to your original rotation or release you. A second reroute is not how Section 23 L. works. Don't acknowledge the CNO — call scheduling and assert your rights.

FAQ

Is PilotForge affiliated with any airline or union?

No. PilotForge is an independent tool built by a pilot. We are not affiliated with any airline, any pilot union, the FAA, or any scheduling provider.

Is this legal advice?

No. PilotForge is an informational reference tool only. All responses cite specific contract sections and regulations, but the PIC is solely responsible for compliance decisions. Always verify with Crew Scheduling.

What about airline IT policies?

PilotForge does not interface with any airline system. It's a BYOD model — you upload your own documents (schedule PDFs, bid packages) that you already have. We don't access any airline applications or systems.
