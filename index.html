What PilotForge Does

📋

Schedule Analysis

Upload your schedule and get instant analysis of trips, rest periods, and duty times.

⚖️

Compliance Tracking

Automatic checks against FAR 117 rest requirements and contractual work rules.

💰

Pay Optimization

Understand premium pay opportunities, green slips, and credit hour calculations.

📊

Bucket Planning

Forward-looking analysis of your rolling limits to plan open time pickups.

💬

WhatsApp Interface

No app to download. Ask questions and upload schedules directly via WhatsApp.

🔒

Private & Secure

Your data stays yours. Per-pilot isolation with no data sharing between users.

About PilotForge

PilotForge is an independent tool built by pilots, for pilots. We are not affiliated with any airline, union, or scheduling provider.

PilotForge provides schedule analysis and decision-support tools. It does not provide legal, regulatory, or operational advice. Always verify compliance with your airline's policies and applicable regulations.
