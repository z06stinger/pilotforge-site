Questions.

How is ZuluRoam different from generic jet lag apps?

Generic jet lag apps generate static plans for tourists. ZuluRoam is built for crew — it factors in report times, in-flight breaks, augmented rest, and multi-leg rotations. You customize your plan with real choices (nap, workout, dinner, drinks) and get adaptive feedback powered by your Apple Watch data.

Do I need an Apple Watch?

No. ZuluRoam works great without one. The Watch adds real-time recovery data (sleep stages, HRV, resting HR) that makes recommendations more personalized, but the core science engine works for everyone.

What airlines does ZuluRoam support?

All of them. The circadian science is universal. ZuluRoam supports FAA, EASA, JCAB, CAAC, and ICAO regulatory frameworks. Schedule photo import currently reads iCrew format, with more formats coming.

Is this medical advice?

No. ZuluRoam provides science-backed recommendations for sleep timing, light exposure, and activity scheduling. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult your AME for health concerns.

What's the relationship between Atlas and ZuluRoam?

Both are built by PilotForge. Atlas is a WhatsApp-based schedule strategy AI — FAR 117, pay, bidding, bucket tracking. ZuluRoam is an iOS app for circadian optimization on layovers. Different tools, same mission: help pilots fly smarter.
