Real questions from real pilots — answered in seconds.

What's my FDP limit for a 3-leg day reporting at 0600?
Per FAR §117.13 Table B, acclimated, 0500-0559 report: 3 segments = 12.0h FDP limit. Extension available per §117.19 up to 2 additional hours with PIC/SIC agreement.
Can I fit a 6-day trip starting May 20 into my bucket?
Day-by-day analysis: your bucket peaks at 98.0h on May 22. The trip fits — 2h margin. Old A157 hours drop off May 2-7, creating room as the new trip's block accumulates.
I was rerouted yesterday. They're trying to reroute me again. Legal?
Per PWA Section 23 L., after a reroute the Company must return you to your original rotation or release you. A second consecutive reroute is not how Section 23 L. works. Don't acknowledge the CNO — call scheduling and assert your rights.

Questions.

Is PilotForge affiliated with any airline or union?

No. PilotForge is an independent tool built by a pilot. Not affiliated with any airline, pilot union, the FAA, or any scheduling provider.

Is this legal advice?

No. PilotForge is informational only. Responses cite specific contract sections and regulations, but the PIC is solely responsible for compliance. Always verify with Crew Scheduling.

Does PilotForge interface with airline systems?

No. PilotForge doesn't connect to any airline system. BYOD model — you upload documents you already have. No access to any airline crew management or scheduling application.

Copilot ($19.99/mo or $199.99/yr): Unlimited everything. Deeper strategy reasoning — bid planning, reserve optimization, green slip timing, pay scenarios. Your active decision partner.
